                          
                          Pets for Sale

                           Type 'List'

Rules: 1. Name your pet on purchase.   Ex. Buy wolf Fang
       
       2. Order pets to do things.     Ex. order fang (or wolf) kill rat
       
       3. Some pets can wield weapons. Ex. order fang wield sword
       
       4. Pets will gain xp for killing mobs. Kill your pet to get
          the accumulated xp. Expect your pet to fight back though!
