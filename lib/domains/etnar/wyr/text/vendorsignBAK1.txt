
You can use these commands for trading:

To see what items are available type:    ask <vendor> to list 
                                         ask <vendor> to browse

To buy an item type:                     ask <vendor> to sell <item>
                                         ask <vendor> to sell <item number>
To get the value of item to sell type:   ask <vendor> to appraise <item>
To sell an item type:                    ask <vendor> to buy <item>
