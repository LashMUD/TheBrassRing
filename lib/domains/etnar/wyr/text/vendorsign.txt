
You can use these commands for trading:

Items available type:       ask <vendor> to list 
                            ask <vendor> to browse
                            ask <vendor> to browse <item(s)>

Buy an item type:           ask <vendor> to sell <item>/<item #>
                             or
                            buy <item>/<item #> from <vendor> 
        
Value of item to sell type: ask <vendor> to appraise <item>

Sell item to vendor type:   ask <vendor> to buy <item>
                             or
                            sell <item> to vendor
